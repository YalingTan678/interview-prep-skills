Foundations
Built on Proven Frameworks

We adapted product strategy frameworks to interview preparation. The same logic that helps companies win customers helps you win offers.

🎯

Jobs to Be Done

Anthony W. Ulwick

The employer has a problem to solve. Understand that problem, and you'll understand what they really need from you.

🔍

Continuous Discovery

Teresa Torres

Research systematically before you "sell." Official sources, previous holders, community platforms, first-hand experience.

💡

Value Proposition Design

Strategyzer

You are the "product." The employer is the "customer." Connect your specific experiences to their specific needs.

♟

Playing to Win

Roger L. Martin

Before deciding how to win, decide where to play. Choose the right positions before preparing for them.

Philosophy
Methods, Not Answers

Teach frameworks

Not: "Here are common interview questions"
Instead: "Here's how to derive the questions yourself"

Interactive, not passive

Not: dump a 10-page guide
Instead: step-by-step with checkpoints

Specific, not generic

Not: "Research the company"
Instead: "Search LinkedIn for previous holders. Note what they did before and after."

Build stories, not scripts

Not: pre-written answers to memorize
Instead: frameworks to think through your own stories
